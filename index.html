Dave is a breath of fresh air

Dave makes going to work fun. I like my job anyway, but Dave makes work even more fun. He remembers things that I forget, and when he forgets something, he's open to correction. He always treats people with respect and answers questions in such a way that students get a light bulb moment.

I've worked with Dave for a short time, but he has been reliable and very helpful. He tries to do his best at all times. Employees can sometimes coast or get distracted, but Dave thinks about the big picture and tries to inject creative solutions wherever he can.
